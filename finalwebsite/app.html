The Isabella Gardner Museum is one of the unique treasures of Boston, the goal of this app is to bring the museum to global users who cannot physically be in the space that is the museum. An alternate way of experiencing this one of a kind museum. The App takes the user through an interactive tour of selected rooms+the courtyard of the museum. By clicking on the close up thumbnails of artworks the user can explore what it is exactly about the museum that makes it so special.
