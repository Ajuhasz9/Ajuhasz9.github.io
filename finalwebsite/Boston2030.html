IMAGINE BOSTON

ANNA
JUHÁSZ

Imagine Boston 2030, a project for the new city plan of Boston for the year 2030. For the reimagining of Boston I focused on the neighborhoods and the people of Boston coming together, connecting to create a brighter future for the city. I wanted to demonstrate a network, I decided to use black thread. Each strand representing one individual idea. With all of these individuals coming together a greater image or vision can be created.
