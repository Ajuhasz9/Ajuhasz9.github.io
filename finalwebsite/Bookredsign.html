Existence is a collection of works by renowned Czech author Milan Kundera. The collection explores the insignificance and fragility of a human beings soul in comparison to the intricate web of the universe and all other life around them. The books were all designed using india ink and water. The ink as it spreads throughout the water creates an almost dream like atmospheric effect. Dala Floda as a serif behaves very much like the characters in Kundera's books, delicate, fragile and almost lost within the universe.
