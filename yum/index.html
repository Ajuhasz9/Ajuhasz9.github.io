Lemon Mousse

Ingredients

  • 1/4 cup dry white wine
  • 1/4 cup sugar
  • 1 1/4 cups heavy cream
  • 2 eggs whites, lemon zest to decorate; thin butter cookies to serve

Preparation

  1. 1. Put the lemon rind and juice, wine and sugar in a bowl. Mix until the sugar has dissolved. Add the cream and beat with a handheld mixer.
  2. 2. Beat the egg whites in a separate bowl, until stiff but not dry gentlyfold cream into mixture.
  3. Spoon the mixture into four serving glasses, decorate with lemon zest and serve.
