Chicken Piccata

Ingredients

  • 2 skinless and boneless chicken breasts
  • sea salt and freshly ground pepper
  • all purpose flour
  • 6 tbsp virgin oil
  • 1/3 cup of fresh lemon juice
  • 1/2 cup chicken stock
  • 1/4 cup brined capers
  • 1/3 cup parsley, chopped

Preparation

  1. 1. Season the chicken with salt and pepper. Dredge chicken in flour and shake off excess
  2. 2. In a large skillet over medium heat, melt 2 tbsp of butter with 3 tbsp of olive oil. When butter and oil start to sizzle add 2 pieces of chicken and cook for 3 mintues. When chicken is brown, flip and cook the other side for 3 more min. Melt 2 more tbsp of butter and add another 2 tbsp of olive oil. add another 2 pieces of chicken and cook in the same manner. Place chicken into a plate when cooked.
  3. Into the pan add the lemon juice, stock and capers. Return all the chicken to the pan and let simmer for 5 min. Remove chicken onto a platter and add remaining butter to the pan and whisk.
  4. Pour sauce over chicken and garnish with parsley
